Pixelize

Hi, I'm Jamie, your friendly neighbourhood pixel artist! Shop some cute stuff I've already made in the shop or let me make something special just for you!. It's not enough for my art to just be "okay", I work my magic on each piece to make it so just looking at it brightens your day!

My love for fiction, especially of the fantastical kind knows no bounds. I love immersing myself in all sorts of anime, book and game worlds along with the stories they tell and I'd love to be involved in bringing fictional worlds to life with my art.

Got a vision? Shoot me an email and let's see what we can create!

Website Development

This website was created using HTML, CSS and Javascript. I do have some experience with VueJS and ReactJS but since this website is quite a small demo and doesn't have a database, I decided to just showcase what I can do with the basics.

I just finished my studies and I know I cannot compete with others when it comes to experience but I hope to inspire confidence with showing the quality I can produce once I do learn how something works.

I like pixel art because it's actually a lot like coding: line by line, pixel by pixel, layer by layer, you build up to something really cool. I want to learn new technologies so that I have more materials I can use to make my websites even cooler or even more efficient.

I must confess that I do not prefer website design over coding but I did design these pages using figma and featured my own pixel art. Fun fact, if you mouseover the images on the first page, I snuck tooltips in with trivia about each art just for fun.
